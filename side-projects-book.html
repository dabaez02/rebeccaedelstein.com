A Children’s Book about Emotions

What would it be like if children were able to identity their emotions and address them?

I wrote a children’s book on how we can spot and manage our feelings, especially when we have negative emotions such as anxiety, sadness, loneliness, and even anger.

By teaching a child these skills at a young age, it will shape the rest of their lives.
